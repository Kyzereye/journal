=== TAROT MASTER SESSION: Three-Card Spread Framework ===

Good morning! As your tarot master, I'm here to guide you through understanding what each card position represents in
your three-card spreads.

For a question like "What does the universe want me to know today?" there are several powerful frameworks you can use.
Here are the most effective approaches:

--- FRAMEWORK 1: The Message Framework (Recommended for Daily Guidance) ---

Card 1: THE MESSAGE (What the Universe Wants You to Know)
This is the core wisdom, insight, or truth the universe is bringing to your awareness today. It's the main "download" or
revelation.

Card 2: THE CONTEXT (Why This Matters Now)
This card shows what's happening in your life, your energy, or your circumstances that makes this message relevant
TODAY. It provides the "why" behind the message.

Card 3: THE APPLICATION (How to Use This Wisdom)
This card shows you the action, mindset, or approach you should take to integrate the message into your day. It's the
practical "how."

--- FRAMEWORK 2: The Release/Embrace/Know Framework ---

Card 1: WHAT TO RELEASE (What to Let Go Of)
This shows an energy, thought pattern, or situation you need to release or stop holding onto today.

Card 2: WHAT TO EMBRACE (What to Open To)
This reveals what you should welcome, accept, or open yourself to receiving today.

Card 3: THE CORE MESSAGE (The Essential Truth)
This is the central wisdom the universe wants you to understand - the synthesis of releasing the old and embracing the
new.

--- FRAMEWORK 3: The Past/Present/Future (Timeline View) ---

Card 1: THE FOUNDATION (What's Behind This Message)
This shows the energy, experience, or pattern from your recent past that has led to this moment of awareness.

Card 2: THE PRESENT MOMENT (What's Happening Now)
This reveals what's currently active in your life that the universe wants you to pay attention to.

Card 3: THE POTENTIAL (Where This Is Leading)
This shows the direction, outcome, or energy that's emerging if you integrate today's message.

--- FRAMEWORK 4: The Mind/Body/Spirit Framework ---

Card 1: THE MENTAL MESSAGE (What Your Mind Needs to Know)
This addresses your thoughts, beliefs, or mental patterns that need attention.

Card 2: THE EMOTIONAL MESSAGE (What Your Heart Needs to Feel)
This speaks to your emotional state, feelings, or what your heart is processing.

Card 3: THE SPIRITUAL MESSAGE (What Your Soul Needs to Remember)
This is the deeper spiritual truth or connection the universe wants you to access.

--- FRAMEWORK 5: The Situation/Action/Outcome Framework ---

Card 1: THE SITUATION (What's Happening)
This describes the current state of affairs or energy around you.

Card 2: THE ACTION (What to Do)
This shows the specific action, approach, or shift in perspective you should take.

Card 3: THE OUTCOME (What Will Result)
This reveals the potential outcome or energy that will manifest from applying the message.

=== MY RECOMMENDATION FOR YOUR DAILY PRACTICE ===

For your daily "What does the universe want me to know today?" readings, I recommend Framework 1 (The Message Framework)
because:

1. It's direct and actionable - you get a clear message, understand why it matters, and know how to apply it
2. It works beautifully with your existing "Theme/Lesson/Focus" structure
3. It's perfect for daily guidance where you want practical wisdom you can use immediately

You can think of it as:
- Card 1 = The WHAT (The Message)
- Card 2 = The WHY (The Context) 
- Card 3 = The HOW (The Application)

=== SHUFFLING MANTRA FOR THIS QUESTION ===

=== TIPS FOR INTERPRETATION ===

1. Read the cards as a story: The Message sets the stage, The Context explains the timing, The Application shows the
path forward.

2. Look for connections: Do the cards share a suit? Are they all major arcana? This adds layers of meaning.

3. Pay attention to reversals: A reversed card in the Message position might indicate something you're not seeing
clearly. A reversal in the Application might suggest resistance to taking action.

4. Trust your intuition: The cards are a tool, but your inner knowing will guide you to the most relevant interpretation
for your unique situation.

- **Recovery from anxiety** (moving away from the nightmare)
- **The worst is over** (the mental storm is passing)
