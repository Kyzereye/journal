### 🔍 Detailed Card Analysis

**1. 6 of Cups (Reversed) - What the Interaction Was About**
   - **General:** Releasing the past, moving forward from nostalgia, not dwelling on old patterns, or connection that isn't based on the past.
   - **In this position:** The moment was about **moving forward—releasing the past, or a connection that points away from nostalgia.** The 6 of Cups upright is childhood, innocent connection, the past. Reversed: not that. The interaction wasn't about revisiting something old; it pointed toward something new—a spark that says "forward," not "back." Or: it was a moment of releasing old patterns of how you connect or what you expect from a look.

**2. The Wheel of Fortune (Reversed) - Will You Meet Again Soon**
   - **General:** Cycles not turning in your favor, delays, resistance, bad luck, or the wheel stuck.
   - **In this position:** Will you meet again soon? **The wheel is reversed—the cycle isn't turning that way right now.** It doesn't say never; it says soon is unlikely. The Wheel Rx suggests delays, resistance, timing not aligned. Your thoughts say yes; the card says the wheel isn't spinning toward that meeting—at least not soon.

**3. Ace of Wands (Upright) - What to Trust (or Not)**
   - **General:** New inspiration, creative spark, the seed of potential, new beginning, taking action.
   - **In this position:** What to trust: **the spark—the Ace of Wands.** Trust the inspiration, the fire the moment lit, the possibility of new action. Your thoughts have betrayed you; the Ace of Wands says trust the spark itself, not the "we'll meet soon" story (Wheel Rx says that's not aligned). Trust the new beginning the moment represented; don't trust the specific outcome or timing. Trust the fire; don't trust the thought that it will manifest a certain way, soon.
