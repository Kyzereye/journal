# 📅 07-10
**Face-to-face talk that drops out of hearing**

## The dream

This was a very short dream. You were looking at someone straight in the face, or maybe they were looking straight at you. They were telling you something, and you could hear the words, but the volume of their words dropped very quickly. You could see that they were still talking to you, trying to tell you something, but eventually you could not hear them.

Given the expression on their face and other body language cues, they thought what they were telling you was important. Or maybe you were not understanding something, or “not getting it,” and they were frustrated by that. These were all thoughts at the time.

The last thought you had was that you were losing your hearing, or that this may be a premonition of the future. You do not really think that now, but it is what you thought at the time.
