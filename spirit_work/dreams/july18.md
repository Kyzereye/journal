# 📅 07-18
**Military mall muster, the wrong count, and following instead of knowing**

## The dream

### The gathering on the second floor

There were a bunch of people gathering for something. They were all military people. Later you figured out that it was the United States and Germany. The location was the second floor of a building that felt a lot like an old 1990s shopping mall: two stories, long walkways, and an opening in the middle where the lower courtyard would be. You were on the top floor, sitting at tables and chairs that had been pushed together quite closely.

There was a lot of detail in the setting — shop signs, things on the walls, doors, railings — but none of it was in super high detail. You could easily tell that a shop sign was a shop sign, but you could not read the actual words.

Everyone was gathered and waiting for some event to start.

### The German officers and the quiet retrieval

A couple of German officers wearing World War II uniforms came up and sat with the group. They were looking for a set of German soldiers. They sat with you so they would not draw attention from outside the group. The whole thing was supposed to be kept quiet. It almost seemed as if these soldiers were not where they were supposed to be, but no one wanted anyone to get in trouble for that.

The German officers called out names, and the soldiers answered, but they did not get up right away. The officers called out the names, and you looked around. You saw three German soldiers left, so you held up three fingers to let the officers know that there were three left. You are not sure why you did this. It ended up that you were wrong, and there were five or six. It was a little hard to tell who the German soldiers were because everyone was wearing uniforms that looked a lot alike.

Once the Germans had gotten up and left, your group was ready to go.

### The meeting place you never fully get

There was a slight bit of anxiety because you were not sure where you were meeting, so you asked a person who you knew would have that information. They never answered you directly. They got on a mic and started to relay the information. You did not get the whole name of the location, but you did hear the number 28.

At one point you thought you knew the location in the waking world. You thought you could see the waking-world location in your mind, but the thought faded, and you found yourself grasping to hold on to the information. So you still did not know where you were meeting.

You were about to ask again when you saw people you thought you knew walking in a direction, so you thought you should just follow them. You were still on the second floor, but you could see them walking across an open lot on the outside of the building, opposite the courtyard. It was at that point that the dream ended.
