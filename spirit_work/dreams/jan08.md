# 📅 01-08
**Dream:** The Tiger in the Background

## Dream Analysis

**The Two People:**
The presence of two people standing together often represents a duality or a partnership within yourself. Your impulse to
help one of them indicates that you are in a supportive, active role regarding your own internal alignment or a
specific connection in your life. It shows a move toward cooperation rather than isolation.

**The Tiger:**
A tiger is a potent symbol of raw power, sovereign strength, and primal instinct. It mirrors the "Strength" card and the
"Porsche" from your recent work—representing a high-performance energy that is now part of your landscape.

**Background and Scale:**
Because the tiger is in the background and appearing smaller, it signifies that this immense power is currently "off-
stage." It isn't the primary focus or the immediate driver of your actions right now. It is present, but it isn't
demanding your attention or threatening your current state. It shows that your power has become a stable part of your
environment rather than an overwhelming force you have to "manage."

**Running Around a Corner (Left):**
The left side is traditionally associated with the unconscious, intuition, and the receptive feminine principle. The
tiger running around a corner suggests this power is moving into your unconscious or intuitive space. It isn't
attacking; it is simply in motion, transitioning into a deeper part of your psyche where it can operate without being
scrutinized by your logical "Page of Swords" mind.

---

## Synthesis
This dream fragment shows that your sovereign power (the Tiger) has moved from being a "project" you need to fix or
manage to being a natural, background presence in your life. You are focused on the human element—helping and
partnership—while your raw power is simply "running" in the background of your unconscious. It is a sign that the
integration we've been discussing is happening quietly, out of sight of your analytical mind.
