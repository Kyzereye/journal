=== DREAM: The Clearance Gate ===

**The Dream:**
There was some travel, not sure where. It seemed to be dark. Not necessarily nighttime, but dark surroundings. There were some large vehicles, SUVs maybe. They were black and I never really remember being in them. But travel seemed to be facilitated by them. 

We got to the clearance check station. There was some question about my clearance to go farther. There was a waiting room, that I was in. It felt like there were more people there, but I never really saw anyone. These would include drivers, although the vehicles that I was in seemed to move by themselves, clearance checking people, other people I was traveling with...

Because I knew my clearance was good, I decided to travel on by myself. The vehicle I was in was white, and I seemed to be in more control of its movements than the other vehicles I was in. It seemed to be a white van that I am driving in the awakened state.

I got to the gate. I could clearly see a gate. One big arm, painted red and white diagonal strips. There was a gatehouse. It was grey and it seemed to be night. I don't know if I got through the gate or not, but there was an active unhappy situation about how my clearance was not good, and I should not be there. It felt like I was being pushed around although not hurt.

I got into some room. There may have been questions, but I don't remember what they were. I felt like I didn't have any answers. There was a bit more to the dream, but it seemed to end there.
