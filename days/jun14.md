**Assistant:** Learning that some people have no inner voice may matter because your whole method assumes a dialogue channel. Your voice is not only the problem when it spirals. It is also the tool you named for steering energy. Cutting off a negative run is not silencing the instrument. It is refusing one tune while keeping the player.

The insult question is not really about weak versus strong. It is about what you are attached to in the moment. If you respond mainly to protect an image of yourself, that can be as sticky as swallowing an insult and stewing on it. Either way the attachment is to how you are seen, not to what is true or useful in the situation.

The roles you listed are different kinds of past. Max is a completed bond. You are not pretending his time did not happen. Runner, rescuer, dog owner — some labels are easier to set down because they do not pull at you daily. The girlfriend attention is harder because it is not only who you were. It is something you still want. Releasing the past there may mean separating the women you dated from the attention you miss, and asking what you want now without requiring those exact faces to supply it.
