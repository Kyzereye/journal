# Journal — 19 July 2026

The idea of keeping moving forward—stop looking in the rearview mirror—has been sticking in my head the last day or so. I think that book will be something that I will be working on more. The point of the book is that we get ourselves stuck on a relationship, project, or life because we are focused on what is behind us more than on the direction we are going. Yes, the direction we are going has its own issues to deal with, but if we are constantly focused on fixing, or complaining about, what is behind us, then we lose that energy for moving forward. Activation energy. We also need to break static friction, which is the force that keeps an object from moving. Do not forget about kinetic and potential energy, as well as inertia, which is an object's resistance to any change in its state of motion. Even rest is a state of motion.
